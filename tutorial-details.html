Tinikling Essentials

Originating from Leyte, Tinikling is a lively folk dance performed between bamboo poles. This tutorial teaches footwork, timing, and cultural context in an easy-learning format.

  • Origin: Leyte
  • Region: Visayas
  • Costume: Baro't saya and camisa de chino
  • Music: Fast-paced rondalla with bamboo rhythm

  • Keep your steps light and controlled.
  • Remember the cultural meaning behind each movement.
