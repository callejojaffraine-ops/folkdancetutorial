Cariñosa is a graceful courtship dance. Sa tutorial na ito, focus sa soft hand movements, fan or handkerchief styling, partner facing, at mahinahong footwork.

Itik-Itik Tutorial

Itik-Itik imitates the playful movement of ducks. Sa bawat figure, bantayan ang quick steps, bent knees, wrist flicks, at masiglang body movement.

Figure 1

Start with the basic duck-like arm movement and small lively steps.
